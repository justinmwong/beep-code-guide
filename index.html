What are beep codes?

Beep codes are auditory signals produced by a computer during the Power On Self Test (POST). They serve as a diagnostic tool to identify hardware issues, particularly when your computer fails to display anything. However, it's important to note that beep codes can vary depending on the motherboard manufacturer and BIOS version installed.
